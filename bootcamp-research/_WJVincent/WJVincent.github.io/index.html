About

Husband, Father, Friend. We are all first defined by those closest to us. I love developing good relationships with those who I work with often, investing in people both for the act itself and for the benefits it has on a working environment and the end product we deliver.

I love tech. I'm fascinated by all of the new shiny ideas that come out. I love to see what the brilliant minds of our generation are coming out with. I love all of the unique frustrations that cause me to wonder why I love tech. I love finally solving that brain bending bug that has had me stuck for what seems like an eternity. Coding satisfies my intrinsic desire to solve creative problems analytically and it is a deeply satisfying process.

My favorite languages and technologies to work with are JavaScript, Python, NodeJS, Express, Flask, PostgreSQL, HTML, and CSS. However I am always dabbling in new technologies and deeply enjoy the process of picking up new tools and learning to use them in my projects!
